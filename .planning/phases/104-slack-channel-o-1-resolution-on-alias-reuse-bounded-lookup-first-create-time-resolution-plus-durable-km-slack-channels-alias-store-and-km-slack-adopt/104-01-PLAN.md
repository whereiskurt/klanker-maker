Land the P0+P1 core of the O(1) Slack channel resolution fix â€” pure Go, no infra. This is the heart of the incident fix: bound `FindChannelByName` (page cap + ctx-per-page + typed `ErrScanCapExceeded`), add the `IsChannelNotFound` classifier so only a definitive `channel_not_found` invalidates a stored mapping, and restructure `resolveSlackChannel` (Mode-2) into a lookup-first, budgeted state machine that NEVER falls through to an unbounded `conversations.list` scan on a transient `conversations.info` blip (the exact defeater that wedged the 900s create-handler).

Output: Bounded `FindChannelByName`, `ErrScanCapExceeded`, `IsChannelNotFound`, and a lookup-first/budgeted `resolveSlackChannel` with `slack_resolve` observability.
