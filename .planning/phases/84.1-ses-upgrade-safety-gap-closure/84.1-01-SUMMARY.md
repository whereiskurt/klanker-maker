## Decisions Made

- **No deprecation shim** (H5 from plan-checker rev 1): a transitional `ExportConfigEnvVars` shim calling through to the new name would add confusion debt for future readers. With only 8 production callers and 4 test references, the mechanical sed rename is cleaner and the grep gate enforces the invariant.
- **KM_REGION_LABEL derived via `compiler.RegionLabel`, not a new config field**: keeps the canonical region-label conversion single-sourced in `pkg/compiler/region.go` and avoids drift between the compiler and the env-var helper.
- **Exported `RunBootstrapSharedSES` forwarder rather than refactoring cobra wiring**: the test seam is a one-line forwarder; the cobra command continues to call the unexported `runBootstrapSharedSES` directly. Minimal blast radius.
- **Strict-superset canary at the workflow level** (`TestRunBootstrapSharedSES_ExportsAllSiteHCLVars`): a single test asserts all 10 site.hcl-consumed env vars are non-empty after `runBootstrapSharedSES` returns. Future contributors who add a new `get_env(...)` call in `site.hcl` will see this test fail until they update both ExportTerragruntEnvVars and the canary's required-vars list — keeping the contract aligned across HCL and Go.
