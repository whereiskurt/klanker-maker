Column alignment math: each label uses 13 chars before the value (label + colon + spaces). Examples:
- `"Status:      "` = 6 chars + ":" + 6 spaces = 13
- `"Created At:  "` = 10 chars + ":" + 2 spaces = 13
- `"Failure:     "` = 7 chars + ":" + 5 spaces = 13 ✓
- `"Failed At:   "` = 9 chars + ":" + 3 spaces = 13 ✓
