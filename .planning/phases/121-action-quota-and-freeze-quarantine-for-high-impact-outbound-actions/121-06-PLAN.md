<objective>
Make the Slack + H1 bridge Lambdas the agent-untrusted chokepoint for chat posts. They hold the
bot tokens, so a throttled sandbox can never silence its own trip notice (CONTEXT.md §5). Three
behaviors: (1) `FetchByChannel` reads `action_limits` + `action_frozen` onto `SandboxRoutingInfo`
(BRG-03); (2) each outbound action (`ActionPost`/`ActionUpload`, `h1_comment`) calls
`quota.Record` and on a BLOCK/FREEZE trip returns 429 + posts an in-thread control-plane notice
(BRG-01/H1-01); (3) inbound dispatch refuses new turns when frozen + posts the frozen notice
(BRG-02). Add the bridge-role IAM for the quota table.

Purpose: Bridges own slack_post/h1_comment (NOT the proxy — Risk 1 double-count). The chat-trip
in-thread notice is the immediate user-facing half of the dual notification model.
Output: Bridges record + enforce + gate-on-frozen; in-thread notices render.
</objective>
