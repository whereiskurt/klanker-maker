CRITICAL FINDING (94-RESEARCH.md Item 1): the per-sandbox Lambda log groups are HARDCODED
with the literal `km-` prefix in Terraform (budget-enforcer/main.tf:260,
github-token/main.tf:159), NOT `${var.resource_prefix}`. On a non-default-prefix install (e.g.
kph) the groups are `/aws/lambda/km-budget-enforcer-{id}`, which is exactly why destroy/ttl
deletes missed them and 271 leaked. The sandbox audit group `/km/sandboxes/{id}/` and the ECS
sidecar group `/km/sidecars/{id}` are likewise hardcoded `/km/`. The check MUST filter on these
literals, not the dynamic prefix.
