Lock a sandbox to prevent accidental destruction or stoppage.
While locked, km destroy, km stop, and km pause are blocked.
Use 'km unlock' to restore normal operations.

Usage:
  km lock <sandbox-id | #number>

Examples:
  km lock #1               Lock sandbox #1
  km lock sb-abc123        Lock by full ID
  km lock #1 --remote      Dispatch lock via Lambda

Flags:
  --remote  Dispatch to Lambda via EventBridge (no local AWS creds needed)
