Extend a running sandbox's TTL.

Usage:
  km extend <sandbox-id | #number> <duration>

Examples:
  km extend #1 2h          Extend sandbox #1 by 2 hours
  km extend sb-abc123 30m  Extend by 30 minutes
  km extend #1 2h --remote Dispatch extend via Lambda

Flags:
  --remote  Dispatch to Lambda via EventBridge (no local AWS creds needed)
