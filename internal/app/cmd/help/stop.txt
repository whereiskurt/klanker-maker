Stop a running sandbox's EC2 instance without destroying infrastructure.
The sandbox can be resumed later or will be destroyed when TTL expires.

Usage:
  km stop <sandbox-id | #number>

Examples:
  km stop #1               Stop sandbox #1
  km stop sb-abc123        Stop by full ID
  km stop #1 --remote      Dispatch stop via Lambda

Flags:
  --remote  Dispatch to Lambda via EventBridge (no local AWS creds needed)
