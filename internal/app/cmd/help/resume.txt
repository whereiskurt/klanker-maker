Resume a paused or stopped sandbox, starting its EC2 instance.

If the sandbox was paused with hibernation, the instance resumes from
its saved RAM state. If it was stopped normally, the instance boots fresh
but preserves its EBS volumes and configuration.

Examples:
  km resume claude-abc123       Resume by sandbox ID
  km resume #1                  Resume by number from km list
  km resume #2 --remote         Dispatch to Lambda via EventBridge
