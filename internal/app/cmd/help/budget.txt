Manage budget limits for sandboxes.

The budget command group provides tools to view and modify budget
allocations for sandbox compute and AI spend. Budgets are tracked
in DynamoDB and enforced by the budget-enforcer Lambda.

Usage:
  km budget <subcommand> [flags]

Available Subcommands:
  add    Add budget (top-up) to a sandbox

Use "km budget <subcommand> --help" for details on each subcommand.
