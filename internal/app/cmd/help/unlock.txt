Unlock a sandbox, allowing destroy, stop, and pause operations.
Requires confirmation unless --yes is passed.

Usage:
  km unlock <sandbox-id | #number>

Examples:
  km unlock #1               Unlock sandbox #1
  km unlock sb-abc123 --yes  Unlock without confirmation
  km unlock #1 --remote      Dispatch unlock via Lambda

Flags:
  --yes     Skip confirmation prompt
  --remote  Dispatch to Lambda via EventBridge (no local AWS creds needed)
